What we do

Intelligence-led advice for aquaculture in transition

ROC combines deep sectoral knowledge with spatial analysis and on-the-ground field experience to deliver advisory products that hold up in the real world — not just on paper.

Sector & Suitability Assessment

Multi-criteria aquaculture suitability modelling using remote sensing, CMIP climate scenarios, and socioeconomic layers — from desk review to field verification.

02
